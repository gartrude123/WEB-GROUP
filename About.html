ABOUT OUR DEPARTMENT

The department of computing and informatic at bugema uniersity typially focusses on

  1. COMPUTING: This includes all computer courses i.e, computer science, information technology, software engineering, data science, Ai, cybersecurity and other related courses. to learn more check here >> Student portal

  2. INFORMATICS: This includes all courses related to information management such as data management, information systems, health informatics,data analysis, and other related courses.

THE MAIN FOCUS AREAS OF THE DEPARTMENT INCLUDE

  • RESEARCH: This includes all research activities related to computing and informatics, such as research projects,publications, and conferences.
  • TEACHING: This includes all teaching activities related to computing and informatics, such as lectures,tutorials, and practical sessions.
  • INDUSTRY PARTNERSHIPS: This includes collaboration with tech companies.
  • ADMINISTRATION: This includes all administrative activities related to computing and informatics, such as budgeting, staffing, and policy development.
