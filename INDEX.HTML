Welcome to the Department

Welcome to the Department of Computing and Informatics at Bugema University. We are one of the leading departments in the School of Computing and Informatics, committed to providing quality education and research opportunities.

Our department offers a range of courses at certificate, diploma, and degree levels, including Information Technology, Computer Science, and Software Engineering. We also provide short courses in web development, data analysis, and digital marketing, among others.

Our Mission

The department is committed to providing quality education and research opportunities for its students and staff. This can be achieved through a combination of efforts by both students and staff working together toward excellence.
